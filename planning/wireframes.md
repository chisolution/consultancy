# Wireframes & User Interface Design

### Design Philosophy
**Approach**: Clean, professional, trustworthy design that conveys expertise while remaining approachable
**Target Audience**: Business owners, entrepreneurs, finance managers across Rwanda, Canada, US, Cameroon
**Key Principles**: Cultural sensitivity, accessibility, mobile-first, performance-optimized
